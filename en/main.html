About Hackcraft

Hackcraft was founded by a group of awesome high school students and collage students, aimed to be one of the most unique and fascinating hackthon ever. Hackthon comes from the U.S. collage as a cool game played by the top coders in the computer science schools. The word "Hack" is a special skill for awesome programmers to create a non reproducible but extremely excellent idea. It’s all about create awesome new things. It’s cool and it’s fun. Linus HACKed and created GNU/Linux system; Bjarne HACKed and created C++; Ryan HACKed and created Node.js. These people would be remember forever as great HACKERS. Why won’t you be the next?
